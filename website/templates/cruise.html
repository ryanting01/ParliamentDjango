Cruise Medicals

We are a provider of employment medical services for Royal Caribbean in Ireland. New employees requiring medical evaluations can book their exams with us. You should allow one and a half hours for the comprehensive medical. to make an appointment and for further guidance related to your exam. What to bring to your medical exam appointment:

  • Your passport or other photo ID.
  • Your glasses or contact lenses.
  • Copies of any significant hospital letters, abnormal smear results etc.
  • Contact us for an appointment.


The medical exam will include the following:



  • Full Blood Count
  • Hepatitis B Antigen
  • Liver Function Tests
  • HIV Test
  • Urea and Electrolytes
  • Liver Function Tests
  • Lipid Profile
  • Blood Glucose
  • Hepatitis A
  • Hepatitis C
  • STD Screening
  • Urine Drug Screen
  • Hearing Test
  • (Pregnancy test if needed)
