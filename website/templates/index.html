Children's Health

Immunisation is a simple safe and effective way of protecting your child against certain diseases. The risks from having these diseases are far greater than the risk of any minor side effects from immunisation. We provide immunizations for your baby as recommended by the HSE

Seafarers Medicals

We offer a comprehensive medical examination for those who require a Seafarer Medical Certificate (ENG-11), mercantile marine, and fishing services requiring an eyesight test certificate are also available. Our medical services are fully approved by the Department of Transport, and we can issue the relevant certificates to you required by law. We also provide 2 year renewal checks. The consultation will include a full medical examination, and you will need to present a photo ID with your previous certificate.

Women's Health

The National Cervical Screening Program provides free smear tests to women aged 25 to 60. A smear test is a simple procedure that only takes minutes and is the most effective way to detect changes in the cells of the cervix. For more information on Cervical Smears please select the "HSE Scheme" button.
