Women's Health

National Cervical Screening

The National Cervical Screening Program provides free smear tests to women aged 25 to 60. A smear test is a simple procedure that only takes minutes and is the most effective way to detect changes in the cells of the cervix. For more information on Cervical Smears please select the "HSE Scheme" button.

To arrange for a Cervical Smear Test with us please contact us with your PPS number and date of birth at Tel: 01.677.9112

Breast Exam

Breast self exams should be part of a regular monthly routine for all women. Regular examinations help you to know what is normal for you and makes it easier to pick up on any changes quickly. Early detection is crucial for effective early treatment. If you are over 40 or in a high risk category it is advisable to have a yearly breast exam by your doctor.

Childhood Vaccinations

Immunisation is a simple safe and effective way of protecting your child against certain diseases. The risks from having these diseases are far greater than the risk of any minor side effects from immunisation. We provide immunizations for your baby as recommended by the HSE at the following intervals:

  • 2 Months
  • 4 Months
  • 6 Months
  • 12 Months
  • 13 Months
